Meet Dr. Monica

Your vet best friend for
the years that matter most.

I'm a veterinarian who fell in love with senior dogs — their wisdom, their slowdowns, the profound bond that deepens when you choose to show up with intention.

I created Senior Dog Revolution because pet parents in these years deserve more than rushed vet appointments and 2am Googling. You deserve a guide, a plan, and a community who truly gets it.

My work is rooted in science and led by heart — because the best senior dog care lives at the intersection of both.
